Our history

The foundation of The Oxford Institutions was laid by the Children's Education Society in 1974. Our respected Chairman, Sri. S. Narasaraju with zealous aspirations, fulfilled his earnest ambition while establishing Children's Education Society and now a galaxy of Educational Institutions pose in his Decorum as the Jewels in a Crown.

He is rightly called a "man of mission" who visualized excellence, perfection and variety. In the process, made great venture in establishing The Oxford Senior Secondary School (Affiliated to C.B.S.E. New Delhi) during 1989-90 with first Principal in charge Mrs. Jacqueline Lawrence. Smt. P. IndiraPrithviraj, the Founder Principal of The Oxford Senior Secondary School in the year 1990-91 is well known for her skill in result oriented approach in getting 100% pass results in the Institution. She with her pivotal force and rich experience gave an impetus for all the allied Institutions of the Oxford to function in "UNISON". Thus claiming a unique and distinct recognition in the educational scenario of Bangalore.



The Oxford Senior Secondary School under the CBSE Scheme was established by the Management in the Year 1990. At present the school has classes from Nursery to Std XII on the CBSE pattern vide aff. No.830028
